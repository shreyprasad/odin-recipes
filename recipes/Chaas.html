Chaas

Description

chaas, also known as buttermilk, is a refreshing Indian yogurt-based drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 cups chilled water
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp black salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • A handful of fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 small piece of ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.
  2. Gradually add chilled water to the yogurt, whisking continuously to combine.
  3. Add roasted cumin powder, black salt (or regular salt), black pepper powder, chopped mint leaves, and chopped cilantro leaves. If desired, add chopped green chili and grated ginger for an extra kick.
  4. Mix well to incorporate all the ingredients.
  5. If using, sprinkle chaat masala on top for additional flavor.
  6. Pour the chaas into glasses, garnish with a few mint leaves, and serve chilled.
