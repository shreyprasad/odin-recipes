Palak Paneer

Description

Palak paneer is a popular Indian vegetarian dish made from soft paneer (cottage cheese) cubes cooked in a smooth, flavorful spinach-based gravy, seasoned with various spices, such as garlic, ginger, garam masala, and cumin. It is typically served with naan, roti, or rice.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 300g spinach, washed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cream (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, blanch the spinach by boiling it for 2-3 minutes. Drain, let it cool, then blend into a smooth puree using a blender or food processor. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  3. Add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until the onions become translucent.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Add green chili (optional), turmeric powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Stir in the spinach puree and 1/4 cup of water, then add salt to taste. Bring to a simmer and cook for 4-5 minutes.
  7. Add paneer cubes, gently stirring to ensure they're covered in the sauce. Cook for an additional 4-5 minutes.
  8. If desired, add 2 tbsp of cream to the mixture and stir well to combine.
  9. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
